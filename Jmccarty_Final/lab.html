Welcome to...

(The dancing-est security lab this side of Bespin)

Lab 194 is home of Owens Campus' best server room (Sorry Mike) and is also a major hub of trans-galactic travel, I mean uh, the college's computer majors.

Lab 194 and the server room in the back serve as a place of learning for students in all three of DelTech's computer programs: Computer Information Systems, Internet Security, and Computer Network Engineering. A lot of classes are held in Lab 194. Those who are in the Lab have access to its server racks, network switches, and virtualized network. All of which are used to host Virtual Machines created by students and teachers for experimentation and class demonstrations.
